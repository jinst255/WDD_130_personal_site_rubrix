Privacy Policy

Last Updated March 4th, 2026

Introduction

Weiss Solutions LLC ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use and share information when you use the Rubrix Chrome extention and website.

The use of information recieved from Google API's will adhere to the Chrom Web Store User Data Policy, inluding the Limited Use requirements

What We Collect

  • Account Information: Your email address when you sign up or sign in
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the extention (features used, actions taken). your email address is anonymized before being sent to our analytics service
  • Canvas Course Data: When you use Rubrix on Canvas, we sync your enrolled course names and IDs to our backend to associate gradin settings with each class
  • Canvas Account Email: When you visit a Canvas page, Rubrix reads your Canvas account email to pre-fill the sign in form and link your gradin history to your Canvas Account
  • Assingment and Submission Content: When you run a grade, the assignment description, rubric, and your submission content are included in the grading prompt sent to AI. This prompt is stored alonside your grade report -- see "Content You Submit" below for more details.

Data Security

All data trasmitted between Rubrix, our backend, and third-party services is sent over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS). Authentication tokens stored locally on your device are managed by Chrome's secure storage API's.

Cookies and Tracking

Rubrix's website does not use cookies or tracking technology. No analytics scripts run on this site.

The Rubrix Chrome extension uses Postlog for anonymized prodcut analytics (see Third-Party Services above). This runs inside the extension context and does not set cookies in your browser

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under GDPR (EU), CCPA (California, USA), or other applicable laws, including the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.
